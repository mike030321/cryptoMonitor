## Approved amendments

### 1. Use 4 roles only

Use:

* `trade`
* `shadow`
* `context`
* `disabled`

Do not use the earlier 6-role schema.

Use subfields instead:

* `context_subkind`: `filter | regime | risk_state`
* `disabled_reason`: `by_data | by_gate | by_operator | by_safety`

### 2. Keep promotion per-slice

A timeframe role is not enough to authorize trading.

Runtime trade authorization must require BOTH:

* the slice is promoted / model available
* the timeframe role is `trade`

### 3. Wait for the campaign verdict before seeding final role values

Do not invent final evidence refs before the report exists.

Wait for #549 / the campaign verdict report, then seed `shared/timeframe-roles.json` with evidence references.

### 4. Meta-brain must understand roles

`slice_role` must be included in the meta-brain telemetry.

The meta-brain must distinguish:

* trade-roled outcomes
* shadow outcomes
* context-only telemetry
* disabled timeframes

Trust updates should count only appropriate trade-roled outcomes.
Context signals may be used for analysis/governance, but not as if they were trade-authority outcomes.

### 5. Do not flip quant on

This design does not authorize turning on `quant_brain_enabled`.

That remains a separate operator decision after evidence and cost review.
