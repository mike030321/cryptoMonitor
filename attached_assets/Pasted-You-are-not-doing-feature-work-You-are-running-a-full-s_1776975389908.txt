Critical audit principle

The audit must focus on the real risks:

Complexity tax
Many interacting systems may hide bugs, stale assumptions, wrong field mappings, and dead wiring.
Governance overfitting
The meta-brain may react to noisy or dishonest telemetry and appear intelligent while learning the wrong thing.
Operational entropy
As the system grows, components may drift out of sync even if local tests still pass.
False confidence from dashboards
I need hard PnL truth, not nice charts or self-reported health.
Required audit style

wrong field semantics
