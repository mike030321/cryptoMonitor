What to tell Replit exactly

Paste this:

Train the quant engine only on real historical market data and real resolved outcomes. Use OHLCV, funding, OI, liquidations, spreads, cross-asset context, and any available microstructure data. For each timestamp, features must use only information available at that time. Targets should include future return, probability TP before SL, MAE, MFE, volatility, and net pnl after estimated costs. No synthetic labels. No leakage. Use walk-forward time-series validation only.
Critical rules (very important)
1. No data leakage

This matters more than anything.

At candle T, model must not see:

future candle highs/lows
future volume
future indicators built using future data

Everything must be point-in-time clean.

2. Walk-forward only

Use:

Train on past → validate on future

Never random split.

3. Costs included

Train/evaluate with:

fees
slippage
spread

Otherwise fake edge appears.
